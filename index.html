I put most of my work in arXiv, figshare or program repositories, because the present publication system breaks science for the sake of monetary benefits and bureaucracy. My analogy is with impressionist movement in art. Back then, academic painters competed hard for public attention by submitting their work to official salons. There was a harsh peer-review system which decided at each heigth the paintings will be exposed, if accepted. The works of academic painters were derisively called "art pompier" because of their uniform style and subjects from a made up antiquity, whith shiny, polished helmets everywhere in sight. The more creative impressionists started to compete for the public attention outside the official channels. There was more variety in their movement, which led to new ideas and developments, out of the pit of art pompier.
